Our Complete Story

Founded in 1983, Robin began as a small weaving workshop in Nairobi with just 15 employees. Our founder, Mr. James Mwangi, believed in the potential of Kenyan craftsmanship to compete on the global stage.

The Early Years

We started by producing traditional kikois and shukas, quickly gaining a reputation for quality and vibrant colors. By 1990, we had expanded to include school wear production, serving local Nairobi schools.

Growth & Expansion

The 2000s saw significant growth as we introduced industrial workwear and expanded our yarn production. We invested in modern weaving technology while maintaining our commitment to traditional craftsmanship.

Community Impact

Today, we employ 750 Kenyans and support local cotton farmers. Our apprenticeship program has trained over 2,000 weavers, preserving traditional skills while embracing innovation.

Every Robin product carries not just quality materials, but 40 years of stories, skills, and Kenyan pride.
